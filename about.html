Unforgettable Experiences

ILS Tours is a company dedicated to incoming tourism ranging from the traditional sightseeing itineraries to very specialized travel opportunities. It was founded in May 2000 in Buenos Aires, Argentina by a Swiss-Argentine couple.

Ongoing commitment to both product quality and service led to success. The ILS family grew into a well-reputed mid-size company with a team of skilled, multilingual experts who strive to provide personalized travel solutions. Frequent familiarization trips upgrade our knowledge about the destinations and maintain an overview about new products.

In 2018 the company became a part of the Travel Services Holding and in 2022 we opened our first local office abroad in Santiago, Chile.

Today we continue to provide excellent service, unique and innovative products to all our clients. We love sharing the very best of each destination with our guests and we are determined to make each trip a memorable experience.

We are the main suppliers to some of the largest travel companies all over the world. Every passenger we receive is treated with extreme care and dedication.
